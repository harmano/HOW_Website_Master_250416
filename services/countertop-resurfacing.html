A Cleaner Approach

Revitalize your countertops, desks, and tables without shutting down your space. Our architectural vinyl resurfacing solution offers a fast, mess-free alternative to replacing high-traffic surfaces—perfect for commercial settings like offices, lobbies, and hospitality venues.

The process is non-invasive and done on-site with minimal disruption. No demolition. No fumes. No downtime. In most cases, your surfaces are ready to use the very next day. Choose from over 1,300 premium finishes—woodgrain, marble, concrete, metal, and more—to match your brand or refresh your interior. The material is fire-rated, stain-resistant, water-resistant, and scratch-resistant, ensuring a clean, modern look that lasts.

It’s the smart, cost-effective way to extend the life and style of your surfaces—without interrupting your business.

Durable & Long-Lasting

Built to last 10-20 years+, architectural vinyl resists stains, scratches, and daily wear—perfect for high-traffic areas with minimal maintenance.

Clean & Odorless Installation

No harsh chemicals, loud construction, or mess—just a fast, quiet, and odor-free transformation with minimal disruption.

Limitless Design Options

Choose from 1,300+ finishes, including wood, marble, steel, and leather, for a premium look that rivals real materials.

Quick & Hassle-Free Application

Resurface doors without removal—install in under 30 minutes per door, reducing downtime and avoiding costly disruptions.

Affordable Alternative to Replacement

Get the high-end look of real wood or stone at a fraction of the cost of new doors, paint, or veneer installations.

Fire-Rated & Anti-Microbial

Meets ASTM E84 Class A & CAN/ULC S102 fire safety standards and features antimicrobial properties—ideal for healthcare and hospitality.

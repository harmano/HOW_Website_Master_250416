Case Study

The goal of the interior design was to create a room that looked modern and was inspired by Southern California living. The designers employed cool and bright tones, such as white and grey marble accents, to achieve this purpose. As you can see in the photos below, these luxury materials blend beautifully in one room, giving it a contemporary feel.

In the living area, the wall behind the sectional sofa is covered with the PM006 Pietra Grey Marble Interior Film. And the opposite wall in the dining area is also amplified by panels decorated with a combination of two film patterns â€“ the same PM006 on the bottom and SMT05 Grey Super Matt Interior Film on the top.

Pietra Grey Marble

A whole chapter should be devoted to the Pietra marble. Because of its deep black hue, it is one of the most famous, opulent, and beautiful stones. The inside film design has striking white veining brilliantly contrasted by a grey backdrop, identical to the original material. In addition, the original material's velvety velour feel is retained in the film pattern. This film is great for generating a variety of environments that are both unique and elegant.

Wood Film

Wall panelling has also been installed in two of the mansion's seven bedrooms. Designers chose to wrap the panels in wood designs to give the sleeping areas a warmer appeal. Both the SPW44 and W873 film patterns reflect various oak tones. A black super matt SMT07 pattern, in addition to wood, is utilised to accentuate the region of electric fireplaces. The film's fire-retardant properties allow it to be used in locations where hot surfaces are in close proximity.

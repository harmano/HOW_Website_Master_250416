Recreating the Look of Real Finishes (waterproof wallpaper)

With the versatility and adaptability of vinyl, you can recreate the look and feel of real finishes at a tenth of the price and time. With unlimited patterns to choose from, clients can recreate the look of real wood finishes, stone, metal, marble and many more.

Because of its simple peel and press installation process, Vinyl Wall Film brings the organic look of wood planking with a faster, more cost effective designer look. With its unique pressure sensitive adhesive, you can reposition or remove the plank without damaging your wall surface. Vinyl Wall Film is water resistant, unlike other wood wall decor that will shift or warp overtime due to moisture. Vinyl Wall Film remains both stain and fade resistant overtime with its protective surface wear layer, so your accent wall will never lose its luster. The surface wear layer is also scratch resistant, giving your walls protection from furniture or other objects that normally damage your walls overtime.

Most buildings need to touch up areas that are scratched or worn down within 5 years of installation, with vinyl however, it is more scratch resistant and can be re-installed at a fraction of the time and cost. Time and money aside, you can also decide to switch from a wood to stone finish, significantly changing the look and feel of a building.
